# Page Override: dashboard

## Purpose
Provide instructors and students a calm operational overview.

## Structure
- Contextual welcome.
- KPI row with consistent card treatment.
- Two-column activity/action area on desktop, stacked on mobile.

## Styling Constraints
- Remove decorative AI badges and flashy gradient strips.
- Keep icon usage informative, not ornamental.
- Prefer neutral surfaces with one accent per interaction zone.
